## Curriculum Alignment - Line Items
STANDARD_1: Standard 1: Career Development and Income (3 chapters) - Career planning, education financing, and income fundamentals with Texas labor market considerations
STANDARD_2: Standard 2: Taxes and Financial Planning (5 chapters) - Federal tax systems, filing requirements, and tax planning (Texas has no state income tax)
STANDARD_3: Standard 3: Banking and Financial Services (2 chapters) - Account types, financial institutions, and banking fundamentals
STANDARD_4: Standard 4: Money Management and Budgeting (6 chapters) - Goal setting, budgeting, saving strategies, and financial decision-making
STANDARD_5: Standard 5: Credit and Debt Management (7 chapters) - Credit fundamentals, borrowing costs, credit scores, loans, and debt management strategies
STANDARD_6: Standard 6: Investment and Wealth Building (7 chapters) - Investment types, risk management, retirement planning, and long-term wealth strategies
STANDARD_7: Standard 7: Insurance and Risk Protection (7 chapters) - Insurance types, risk management, coverage analysis, and protection strategies
STANDARD_8: Standard 8: Consumer Rights and Responsibilities (4 chapters) - Consumer protection, fraud prevention, financial responsibility, and taxation fundamentals
